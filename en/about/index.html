About Us

Chum is an English word that means a close friend. It is Chum‚Äôs wish to become a reliable friend of young people in their development journey.

Established in 2015, Chum is a Vietnamese non-profit organization that seeks to empower kids and young people socially and emotionally to reach their full potential through mentoring relationships and mindfulness-based social & emotional learning.

Chum believes that each young person should be empowered to become the best version of themselves.

VISION

Youths aspire to bring out the best in them and better serve the community.

VALUES

  • Commitment
  • Continous learning
  • Compassion
  • Gratitude

MISSION

Empower and strengthen kids and youths socially, mentally and emotionally via developing their social and emotional skills, philanthropy and mindfulness.
